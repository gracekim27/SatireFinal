Gened 1010: Satire Final Project

For our final project, we decided to make a game to represent what we learned in class about the Carnival. In particular we wanted to develop a game that focused on the subversive role of the carnival. In this game, you are a peasant who finds themself enjoying the local carnival where you start to amass money, power and popularity. As the local government discovers what is going on, they send soldiers to shut down the carnival. It's your job to fight them off and let the carnival continue! Collect money by killing the soldiers and buy upgrades by walking into the carnival tent. We hope you enjoy our game!
